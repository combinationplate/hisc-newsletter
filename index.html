Regulatory Corner

Texas PT license renewals:don't leave CE to August.

Texas Board of Physical Therapy Examiners renewals are on a biennial cycle tied to birth month, and staff whose renewals fall this summer often scramble in the last six weeks. If you have PTs or PTAs on your team, now's a good window to map remaining hours and assign group seats before the rush.

Tip of the Month

Block protected learning hours.

CE that's expected to happen between patient visits doesn't happen. The organizations with the highest completion rates put a two-hour learning block on staff calendars — like PTO, but for education.

Most of our courses run 1-2 hours, which fits neatly into a blocked slot without additional overhead.
